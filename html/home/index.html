About TFI

TFI (Tech Founder Institute) is one of the largest and most successful startup accelerator programs in Southeast Asia, organized by TFI Group, a member of the Founder Institute network (Silicon Valley, USA) since 2011. After 7 programs held in Vietnam and 1 program in Thailand, TFI has helped launch more than 80 graduate startups with a total investment of more than 40 million USD and an estimated total valuation of more than 300 million USD. Some of their most outstanding startups are Appota, Beeketing, Logivan, Hoayeuthuong, Kyna... TFI's goal is to contribute to the formation of a startup ecosystem for Vietnam and help Vietnamese startups develop not only domestically but also globally.

About HUB

HUB is a blockchain-oriented venture capital founded by reputable blockchain entrepreneurs. Our mission is to incubate and invest in emerging startups capable of becoming the next blockchain unicorns. In Vietnam, HUB aims to become the Community Sandbox for blockchain startups where companies can source liquidity, find mentorship, and allow regulators to monitor the development of this industry without risk missing out on its enormous growth opportunity.
